Scan the Board Bored QR (or grab the paper map) → see what's open tonight → walk.

## For businesses
Listings are **curated** — every business on the map comes through us.
One email starts it: **boardquestionmark@gmail.com** (or tell us in person at the walk).
No app, no dashboard homework, and cash-only shops are welcome — we never touch
your register.
